Our Services

From start to finish, we handle all your excavation needs on Maui.

Site Grading

Precision grading for building pads, driveways, and drainage. We ensure proper slope and compaction for lasting results.

Trenching

Utility trenches for water, sewer, electric, and drainage lines. Accurate depth and grade every time.

Land Clearing

Brush removal, tree clearing, and vegetation management to prepare your land for construction or agriculture.

Demolition

Safe and efficient demolition of structures, concrete slabs, foundations, and other obstacles on your property.

Hauling & Removal

Dirt, rock, concrete, and debris hauling. We keep your jobsite clean and materials moving.

Residential Excavation

Foundation digging, backfill, retaining walls, and drainage solutions for homes across Maui.

Septic Systems

Excavation for septic tank installation, leach fields, and cesspool work. Done right, done affordable.

Roadwork & Driveways

Road grading, driveway construction, and access road building for rural and residential properties.
